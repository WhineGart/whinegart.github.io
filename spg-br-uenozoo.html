Ueno Park Zoo

Foreign Branding Project

Ueno Zoo is a popular zoo in Japan. I was given the prompt to redesign the branding for the zoo, so I created logos, ad materials, exhibit materials, and much more than what is shown here! It was a fun challenge to research the design values of such a different culture from my own, in hopes of creating something the actual Japanese public would like.
